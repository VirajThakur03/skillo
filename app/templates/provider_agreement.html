Provider Agreement

Standards for independent professionals operating on Sklio.

Providers use Sklio as a managed marketplace, not as an employment contract by default. This agreement sets expectations around verification, conduct, commissions, payouts, and customer data handling.

Commercial terms

  • Category-based commission applies to completed bookings
  • Net payouts are reduced by platform commission, taxes, and valid holds
  • Payout schedule and dispute holds are shown inside the provider dashboard

Trust and verification

  • Age 18+ required for providers
  • ID, selfie or liveness, and bank verification required before going live
  • Skill proof or certification may be mandatory for regulated or high-risk categories

Conduct standards

  • No harassment, discrimination, abuse, or threats
  • No off-platform payment diversion or private lead capture
  • No fake reviews, identity sharing, or manipulated availability

Independent contractor status

Providers choose when they work, which categories they offer, and how they perform services. Nothing in this agreement should be interpreted as a default employment relationship.

Customer data handling

Phone numbers, addresses, and job details may be used only for booking fulfilment, safety, invoicing, and issue resolution. Reuse for unrelated marketing or harassment is prohibited.

Background and conduct checks

By using the provider side of Sklio, the provider consents to verification, fraud screening, quality audits, and enforcement review where marketplace trust is at risk.

Termination triggers

Sklio may suspend or terminate a provider for identity fraud, repeated misconduct, off-platform diversion, poor quality patterns, unsafe behavior, fake reviews, or repeated late cancellations.
