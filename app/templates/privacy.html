Privacy Policy

What data Sklio collects, why it is used, and how long it stays.

Sklio uses personal data to authenticate users, match services, process payments, investigate disputes, prevent fraud, and comply with Indian legal requirements.

DPDPA-aligned operating model Last updated: {{ legal_last_updated }}

Data collected

  • Account data: name, phone number, email, role
  • Booking data: service, address, schedule, notes, invoices
  • Trust data: identity verification results, selfie or liveness outcome, review history, dispute evidence
  • Operational data: chat, live tracking pings, notification preferences, device and login signals
  • Payment data: provider references, settlement references, and wallet credits, but not full raw card storage

Retention model

  • Booking, invoice, and settlement records: 8 years
  • Chat and dispute evidence: 3 years after booking close unless legal hold applies
  • Raw location pings: 90 days
  • Successful verification media: 180 days unless longer retention is required
  • Failed verification media: 90 days unless abuse review requires extension

User rights

  • Access your data summary
  • Correct or complete your information
  • Request erasure when retention is no longer legally required
  • Withdraw consent for consent-based processing
  • File a grievance through the published redressal channel

Third-party sharing

Sklio shares limited data with specific trusted partners to operate our services. This includes Stripe for payment processing and fraud prevention, OpenAI for AI-assisted chat and matching, cloud infrastructure vendors, identity verification providers, and analytics tools. We also share data with legal authorities where required by law.

Data localization and storage

Primary operational storage for user identity, provider verification, booking records, disputes, and payment-system-linked data should remain on Indian infrastructure. Payment system data must follow RBI localization requirements.

Security model

Sklio uses authenticated sessions, role-based access, audit logs, rate limits, payout holds, and fraud review queues to reduce misuse and unauthorized access.

How to contact us

Privacy or data-rights requests may be filed through the grievance process or by contacting {{ grievance_email }}. Standard acknowledgement should be immediate or same day, with a human review target within 72 hours for standard requests.
