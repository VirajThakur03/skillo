Common Recovery

Exact next steps when something fails.

  • Payment failed: Your payment did not go through. Your slot is not confirmed yet.
  • Upload failed: We couldn't upload that file. Check your connection or try a smaller file.
  • Session timeout: For your security, you were signed out. Please log in again.
  • Network error: The connection dropped. We could not complete that action.

Frequently Asked Questions

Top issues we should answer before support is needed

What happens if a provider declines my booking?

You will see similar providers and a structured quote option instead of losing your job details.

Can I still book if I deny location access?

Yes. Enter your area manually and continue with the same search and booking flow.

When does chat become private?

Public skill rooms are available before booking. Booking rooms become private to the seeker and assigned provider.

How do refunds work?

Refund timing depends on when the booking is cancelled and whether the job is disputed. The full matrix is published in Refund Policy.

When can a provider go live?

After identity verification, bank setup, availability publication, and agreement acceptance are complete.

What if a dispute needs legal escalation?

Use the grievance route for legal or trust escalations. Standard complaints are targeted for acknowledgement within 24 hours.
