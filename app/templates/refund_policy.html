Refund Policy

Clear refund and cancellation outcomes for every major booking scenario.

Refunds are tied to timing, job state, and evidence. The matrix below is designed to keep both seekers and providers protected while reducing avoidable support friction.

Scenario Seeker outcome Provider outcome Platform SLA
Cancel more than 24 hours before service 100% refund No payout Refund initiated within 1 hour
Cancel 2 to 24 hours before service Refund minus 10%, minimum INR 100 No payout Refund initiated within 1 hour
Cancel less than 2 hours before service Refund minus 25%, minimum INR 250 No payout Refund initiated within 1 hour
Provider cancels before service 100% refund plus INR 100 goodwill credit No payout and reliability impact Refund initiated within 1 hour
Provider no-show 100% refund plus INR 150 goodwill credit No payout Initial review within 4 hours
Seeker no-show after provider arrival No automatic refund Visit-fee or protected minimum may apply Decision within 24 hours after evidence review
Disputed incomplete or poor work Partial or full refund based on evidence Partial, held, or zero payout depending on outcome Initial review in 24 hours, final within 5 business days

When refunds may be delayed

Refunds may enter a pending state if the payment provider is slow, a dispute is open, or additional evidence is required.

Dispute-first jobs

For contested service quality, Sklio may offer rework, partial refund, or full refund depending on category, risk, and documented evidence.

Off-platform payments

If users move payment off-platform, Sklio may be unable to provide refund protection, support-backed evidence, or goodwill credits.
