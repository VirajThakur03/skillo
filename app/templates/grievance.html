Step 1: Submit complaint

The complaint may be filed through booking support, dispute submission, or a designated grievance contact flow.

Step 2: Acknowledge receipt

Acknowledgement should be sent within 24 hours with a complaint reference number and next-step summary.

Step 3: Review evidence

Relevant evidence may include chat logs, call notes, booking timeline, location pings, photo uploads, refunds, payout state, and verification records.

Step 4: Issue resolution

Standard grievances should be resolved within 15 days. Safety, identity, or legal escalation cases may require restricted accounts or temporary payout holds during review.
