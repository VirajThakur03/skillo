Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and tracking technologies.

This policy explains what cookies are, how Sklio uses them, and your choices regarding these technologies.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences, and understand how you interact with our platform.

2. How we use cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function properly, including authentication and security features.
  • Analytics & Performance: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve the user experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

3. Your Choices

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie consent banner or your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of the Sklio platform.
