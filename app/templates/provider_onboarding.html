Live Definition

When is a provider live?

A provider becomes live once identity, bank setup, availability, and provider agreement acceptance are complete.

Activation Milestone

What counts as activated?

A provider is activated when the first job is accepted within 14 days of going live.

Soft Launch

Searchable before bookable.

Profiles can be visible before they are bookable, but only fully verified providers should enter live booking and payout states.

Screen Flow

Recommended provider journey

  1. Role choice and secure login.Start with mobile OTP and route into the provider lane immediately.
  2. Profile setup.Capture categories, experience, languages, photos, and service areas before discovery begins.
  3. Identity verification.Collect PAN, one government ID with DOB, selfie, and liveness before any live booking eligibility.
  4. Skill verification.Require licenses or certificates where categories carry higher trust or safety risk.
  5. Bank setup and payout readiness.Complete payout information before the provider can go live.
  6. Availability and pricing.Publish real weekly hours, blackout dates, and base pricing so search and booking use accurate signals.
  7. Go live and first-job coaching.Providers should immediately see quotes, alerts, dashboard coaching, and the next action needed to win the first booking.

Required documents

PAN, one government ID with DOB, selfie or liveness, bank verification, and category-specific certification where required.

Earnings framing

Use a simple calculator during onboarding so providers understand potential gross income before they finish verification.

Drop-off mitigation

ID verification, skill proof, and bank setup are the most likely drop-off points, so each needs explanation, save-and-return, and recovery support.
